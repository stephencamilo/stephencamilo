### Bio

**2007 to 2014: Discovering technology as a business tool**

My journey began in 2007 with hardware and network support, where I learned the fundamentals of how technology connects people. In 2009, I joined a marketing agency and delved into SEO and front-end. By 2014, I had expanded into PHP and WordPress, discovering that websites are strategic tools. Each project solidified my view: technology only makes sense when it solves real business problems.

**2015 to 2019: Immersion in complex architectures**

This period was marked by accelerated technical growth. I worked on increasingly challenging projects, diving into robust architectures and deep integrations. In 2019, I was recognized for my analytical potential and encouraged to pursue a postgraduate degree in Data Science. This step broadened my ability to think structurally and create intelligent automations that elevate any solution.

**2019 to 2025: Consolidation at scale**

I immersed myself in the Drupal ecosystem with companies like Jobsity and CI&T, contributing to high-impact enterprise projects. Complex migrations and large-scale architectures became my daily routine. This technical maturity allowed me to return to WordPress with a more sophisticated perspective, combining the best of both worlds to deliver complete and innovative solutions.

**2026 and the present: The integration between code and strategy**

I developed my own projects that combined technical excellence with business vision. It was there that the greatest lesson came: the most brilliant architecture needs acquisition and retention strategy to generate results. Today, I deliver solutions where code and the digital funnel are born together, integrated. My journey proves that technology and business are a single creative force.
